How to Play Magical Arena

Objective: Reduce your opponent’s health to zero.

Player Attributes

  • Health: Life points; if it reaches zero, you lose.
  • Strength: Determines defending power.
  • Attack: Determines attacking power.

Game Setup

  • Player A: 50 health, 5 strength, 10 attack.
  • Player B: 50 health, 5 strength, 10 attack.

Gameplay

  • Turn-Based Combat: Players attack in turns.
  • Dice Rolls: Each player rolls a six-sided die.
  • Attack Damage: Attack value * attack die roll.
  • Defending Strength: Strength value * defense die roll.
  • Health Reduction: Excess damage (Attack Damage - Defending Strength) reduces the defender’s health.

Example Turn

Player A attacks: Rolls 5. Player B defends: Rolls 2.

Attack Damage = 10 * 5 = 50

Defending Strength = 10 * 2 = 20

Player B’s health: 100 - (50 - 20) = 70

Player B attacks: Rolls 4. Player A defends: Rolls 3.

Attack Damage = 5 * 4 = 20

Defending Strength = 5 * 3 = 15

Player A’s health: 50 - (20 - 15) = 45

Winning the Game

The game ends when a player's health reaches zero. The remaining player is the winner.
